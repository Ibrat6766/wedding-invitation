The Journey

Milestones of Our Love

Spring 2019

The First Meeting

Florence, Italy. A spring exhibition at Galleria dell'Accademia. Isabella was laughing at a painting's placard — Alexander was three steps behind, reading the same sign. He asked if he was missing something. She said everything. They talked for three hours.

Summer 2019

First Date Under the Stars

A rooftop dinner in Rome, late June, when the city glows gold at dusk. Over wine and pasta, they discovered matching tastes in music, contradictory opinions on dessert, and an identical scar from childhood bicycles. Three hours turned to midnight.

Winter 2020

Through Harder Times

The world slowed down, but their love grew faster. Shared balconies became their theatre, video calls their stage. Distance taught them that home isn't a place — it's a person. Alexander moved to Milan by autumn.

Autumn 2022

Adventures Together

Two years of travelling — Santorini cliffs, Lisbon alleyways, Tokyo dawn markets. Each city taught them something new about the other, and every sunset felt like the best one yet. They were building a world of two.

June 2024

He Asked. She Said Yes.

Lake Como, the exact terrace where they had their third anniversary dinner. At sunset, with the water turned amber and the mountains silhouetted, Alexander knelt down. Isabella was already crying before he said a word. The answer was always yes.
